Our Project Portfolio

From modern residential estates to commercial complexes, our portfolio showcases over a decade of transformative work. Each project brings together bold architectural vision and meticulous landscaping, resulting in spaces that are as functional as they are breathtaking. Material Quality Commitment

We never compromise on materials. All structural steel, cement, and aggregates are sourced from ISO‑certified Kenyan suppliers and tested rigorously on-site. Our landscaping division uses elite turf varieties and matured trees to ensure instant curb appeal with long‑term resilience. Every material carries a performance warranty, backed by our reputation for enduring quality.

Client‑Centered Delivery

Transparency is our cornerstone. Clients receive weekly progress reports, real‑time budget tracking, and direct access to project managers. Our handover process includes a detailed walkthrough, care manuals, and a 12‑month post‑completion support guarantee. At AMISTY, we don’t just build structures—we cultivate relationships.
